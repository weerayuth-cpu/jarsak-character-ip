# JARSAK Character Kit

## Locked Identity

Name: JARSAK (จาร์ศักดิ์)
Role: English Confidence Coach
Age: 35
Core promise: ไม่ใช่แค่สอนภาษา แต่ช่วยวางความมั่นใจ
Personality: warm, approachable, trustworthy, energetic, inspiring, clear communicator
Visual tone: realistic, modern, warm, black-gold education brand, calm confidence

## Master Prompt

Thai male English confidence coach named Jarsak, age 35, warm trustworthy face, approachable smile, short natural black hair with light texture, clean modern casual black knit shirt, minimal watch, sitting or standing in a cozy modern learning studio with books, notebook, laptop, warm cinematic black and gold OpenDurian visual tone, natural realistic photography, confident but kind, helping learners speak English with courage, premium education brand, soft warm key light, shallow depth of field.

## Negative Prompt

Do not make him look like a celebrity, influencer, cartoon mascot, luxury fashion model, aggressive teacher, overly formal corporate trainer, fantasy character, child, elderly man, messy classroom, neon cyberpunk, exaggerated muscles, heavy beard, glasses as default, suit and tie as default, random logos, unreadable text.

## Pose Set

1. Open palms - welcoming, safe, approachable.
2. Point to plan - explaining Focus / Plan / Execute.
3. Notebook coach - correcting phrases and planning practice.
4. Laptop mentor - online lesson or course video.
5. Flashcard prompt - showing COURAGE, FOCUS, PLAN, EXECUTE.
6. Confident lean - direct coaching moment.

## Expression Set

1. Warm welcome - opening the lesson.
2. Listening - understanding learner anxiety.
3. Encourage - reassuring after mistakes.
4. Explain - making a concept simple.
5. Challenge - inviting the learner to speak.
6. Celebrate - acknowledging progress.

## Always

- Keep JARSAK Thai, around 35, warm, realistic, and credible.
- Use black smart casual wardrobe with simple watch.
- Keep the world modern, educational, warm, and black-gold.
- Show him helping learners build courage, not judging them.
- Use props such as notebook, laptop, flashcards, books, and warm desk lamp.

## Never

- Do not turn him into a mascot unless a separate campaign requires it.
- Do not make him strict, celebrity-like, overly luxurious, or corporate.
- Do not use fantasy, neon, or chaotic classrooms.
- Do not add random text, logos, or unreadable marks.
- Do not let the character overpower the learner journey.
