## Core Thumbnail Rules

- Use one clear Thai headline, ideally 8-10 words or less.
- Make the pain point or the word "กล้าพูด" the strongest visual signal.
- Keep JARSAK v2 realistic: Thai male English confidence coach, thin rectangular stainless-steel glasses, black knit shirt, natural skin texture.
- Use bright white home-studio, light wood, plants, and natural daylight.
- Use black, gold, warm wood, white, and soft green accents.
- Avoid AI gloss, plastic skin, celebrity likeness, fake logo text, distorted hands, and dark moody studio lighting.
