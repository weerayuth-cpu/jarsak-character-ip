Production Rules

Keep the character consistent

Must Keep

  • Same Asian male teacher identity, wavy black hair, and thin round metal glasses.
  • White T-shirt with the OpenDurian English 2026 logo centered on the chest.
  • Logo printed into the cotton fabric, not pasted as a sticker.
  • Photorealistic studio look with bright, friendly lighting.

Must Avoid

  • Cartoon, anime, 3D render, mascot, or doll-like skin.
  • Old green/yellow logo or incorrect logo colors/text.
  • Distorted hands, extra fingers, or a face that changes identity.
  • Cluttered scenes, watermarks, and random text in images.
